About

Here is our reason for creating this site and company

The original American Bubble Waffle was based on the street food craze known as "egg waffles" in Hong Kong. With a special waffle iron and multiple waffle batter flavors, "egg waffles" quickly became a popular snack/breakfast item. After millions on pintrest, instagram and webblog posts, Bubble Waffes made its way to America, making it a delicious international treat.
While "egg wafles" were normaly served plain or with a variety of fruit, Bubble Waffles serves these tasty treats as an icecream cone with homemade creamy icecream and toppings. A classic dish trasformed into a new food craze that anyone can enjoy.
