Bubble Waffles are delicious baked special waffle cones filled with your favorite classic icecream flavors

What is a Bubble Waffle Cone?

Does this trendy new ice cream cone look like bubble wrap to you? Of Course It Does! Don't you worry. Our egg waffles have the same awesome waffle taste you love but in our fun new bubble waffle makers. Instead of a Belgian waffle grids, our bubble waffle looks like some sort of interconnected hive of light, airy, waffle spheres. Instead of the boring old flavourless cone, we can make the batter complement the flavour of ice cream and millions of toppings.

IceCream

We can personalize your bubble waffle batter to your liking. Only served cold, you can customize your flavors. From fruity strawberry to tropical coconut, valentine chocolate or even wild red bean.
